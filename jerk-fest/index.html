We’ve made a place which has a special atmosphere with old soul of skatebording. The main idea of creation was a wish to have a place where everyone can come without shame and enjoy something that he/she has never tried before. The festival is mobile — it changes a location every weekend — and, also, it works with no limited time. One of the key benefits is skate zone: it is not typical area which you used to see — it is the street geometry where you will feel a magnificent feelings of skating.

MAC DEMARCO
Canadian multi-instrumentalist and multimedia artist Mac DeMarco first emerged from complete obscurity under the moniker Makeout Videotape. Immediately after finishing high school in the summer of 2008, DeMarco moved from his hometown of Edmonton to the warmer climes of Vancouver, and self-released Heat Wave, a collection of songs he wrote and recorded while bored and listless in his new surroundings.
