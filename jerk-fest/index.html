We’ve made a place which has a special atmosphere with old soul of skatebording. The main idea of creation was a wish to have a place where everyone can come without shame and enjoy something that he/she has never tried before. The festival is mobile — it changes a location every weekend — and, also, it works with no limited time. One of the key benefits is skate zone: it is not typical area which you used to see — it is the street geometry where you will feel a magnificent feelings of skating.

HEADLINERS

MAC DEMARCO

Canadian multi-instrumentalist and multimedia artist Mac DeMarco first emerged from complete obscurity under the moniker Makeout Videotape. Immediately after finishing high school in the summer of 2008, DeMarco moved from his hometown of Edmonton to the warmer climes of Vancouver, and self-released Heat Wave, a collection of songs he wrote and recorded while bored and listless in his new surroundings.
DIIV

DIIV is an American rock band from Brooklyn, New York City, formed in 2011. The band consists of Zachary Cole Smith (vocals, guitar), Andrew Bailey (guitar), Devin Ruben Perez (bass), Colin Caulfield (keyboards, guitar) and Ben Newman (drums).
TRUST

TR/ST (previously also known as Trust) is the dark electropop project of Robert Alfons from Toronto, Canada. It began as a band in 2010 when Alfons met Maya Postepski of Austra.
FOXYGEN

Foxygen is an American indie rock duo from Westlake Village, California, formed in 2005.[1] The band consists of Jonathan Rado and Sam France.[2] They have released four albums and a number of self-released EPs.

STARS

LEO ROMERO
Leo Romero (born November 28, 1986[1]) is a professional skateboarder whose early skateboarding influences included Jamie Thomas and Andrew Reynolds.
JAMIE THOMAS
Jamie Thomas (born October 11, 1974), is an American professional skateboarder and skateboard industry entrepreneur. Thomas is the owner and founder of Zero Skateboards and Fallen Footwear. Thomas' nickname in the skateboard industry is "The Chief"
David Gonzalez
David González (born 9 August 1990) is a Colombian professional skateboarder. In December 2012 he was named Thrasher magazine's "Skater of the Year".
